TAVERN PACK - FREE DEMO
Low Poly Modular Building Kit


WHAT'S INCLUDED:
- Stone wall sections (plain, window, door, corner)
- Wooden and stone floor tiles
- Wooden door
- Stone pillar


TECHNICAL INFO:
- File Format: FBX
- Scale: 1 unit = 1 meter
- Pivots: Walls pivot at bottom corner for easy snapping and rotation
- Poly Count: Low poly, optimized for games
- Compatible with: Unity, Unreal, Godot, and most game engines


HOW TO USE - MODULAR BUILDING SYSTEM:
- Wall pieces are designed to snap together seamlessly
- Rotate walls at the corner pillar pieces to create angles and turns
- Floor tiles align with wall bases for perfect fit
- Stack roof sections on top of walls
- Mix and match pieces to create unique building layouts


TIPS:
- Use snap-to-grid in your engine for easiest placement
- Corner pillars act as rotation points for creating L-shapes, corners, etc.
- All pieces share the same height/width for consistent modularity
